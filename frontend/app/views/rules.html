What are the rules?

1. The song texts and the execution of the challenge shall have a clear connection to Netlight's employees and/or our values as a company.

2. The songs shall not "compete" with You're the inspiration that we play during our award ceremonies.

3. Everything that is created internally shall be in a state that it does not give any kind of bad publicity if it is spread externally.

4. Due to legal reasons covers are not accepted which means that both music and lyrics must be created by the contestants or that music provided on the contest website is used and lyrics are written for this music.

5. Lyrics must be written in English.

6. You can compete alone or in a group and the group can be as big as you want.

7. Songs must be uploaded before 23rd of mars at 12:00.
